How do I contact you about bringing my team?

There is a map on the contact page with our address. We will also send you more specific directions after you contact us.

What size teams can you accomodate?

We can accomodate teams of 4 - 15 people at once.

We do have a computer room that has wifi, in case of emergencies. We understand that you have a business to run and have to check-in sometimes. We want you to be worry free
